# SASB Standards -- Oil & Gas: Exploration & Production (EM-EP)

## GHG Emissions

Exploration and production companies shall disclose their greenhouse gas emissions management approach and performance metrics.

### Gross Global Scope 1 Emissions (EM-EP-110a.1)

The entity shall disclose its gross global Scope 1 greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions to the atmosphere of the seven GHGs covered under the Kyoto Protocol â€” CO2, CH4, N2O, HFCs, PFCs, SF6, and NF3.

Emissions shall be calculated in accordance with published GHG Protocol standards (Scope 1).

### Amount of Gross Global Scope 1 Emissions from Methane (EM-EP-110a.2)

The entity shall separately disclose methane emissions. Methane is a potent GHG with a global warming potential significantly higher than CO2 over a 20-year time horizon.

### Percentage of Gross Global Scope 1 Emissions Covered Under Emissions-Limiting Regulations (EM-EP-110a.3)

The entity shall disclose the percentage of its gross global Scope 1 GHG emissions that are covered under an emissions-limiting regulation or program that is intended to directly limit or reduce emissions, such as cap-and-trade schemes, carbon tax/fee systems, and other emissions control mechanisms.

## Air Quality

### Air Emissions of Pollutants (EM-EP-120a.1)

The entity shall disclose its air emissions of the following pollutants: NOx (excluding N2O), SOx, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and particulate matter (PM10).

## Water Management

### Total Fresh Water Withdrawn (EM-EP-140a.1)

The entity shall disclose the amount of water, in thousands of cubic meters, that was withdrawn from freshwater sources including surface water, groundwater, rainwater, and municipal water supply.

### Volume of Produced Water and Flowback Generated (EM-EP-140a.2)

The entity shall disclose the volume of produced water and flowback fluid generated from its operations, in thousands of cubic meters.

### Percentage of Hydraulically Fractured Wells with Disclosure of Chemicals (EM-EP-140a.3)

The entity shall disclose the percentage of hydraulically fractured wells for which there is public disclosure of all fracturing fluid chemicals used.

## Biodiversity Impacts

### Description of Environmental Management Policies for Active Sites (EM-EP-160a.1)

The entity shall describe its environmental management policies and practices for active sites in or near areas of protected conservation status or endangered species habitat.

### Number and Aggregate Quantity of Reportable Spills (EM-EP-160a.2)

The entity shall disclose the number and aggregate quantity of reportable spills, including the volume recovered.

## Security, Human Rights & Rights of Indigenous Peoples

### Percentage of Proved and Probable Reserves in Countries with Governance Issues (EM-EP-210a.1)

The entity shall disclose the percentage of its proved and probable reserves in or near areas of conflict or in countries that have the 20 lowest rankings in the Corruption Perceptions Index.

## Community Relations

### Discussion of Engagement Processes and Operating Practices (EM-EP-210b.1)

The entity shall discuss its process for managing risks and opportunities associated with community rights and interests.

## Workforce Health & Safety

### Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR) (EM-EP-320a.1)

The entity shall disclose its total recordable incident rate (TRIR) for full-time employees and contract employees.

### Fatality Rate (EM-EP-320a.2)

The entity shall disclose its fatality rate for full-time employees and contract employees.

## Reserves Valuation & Capital Expenditures

### Sensitivity of Hydrocarbon Reserve Levels to Future Price Projections (EM-EP-420a.1)

The entity shall disclose estimated proved reserves and the sensitivities of those estimates to various future price projection scenarios.

### Estimated Carbon Dioxide Emissions Embedded in Proved Hydrocarbon Reserves (EM-EP-420a.2)

The entity shall disclose the estimated CO2 emissions embedded in its proved hydrocarbon reserves.

## Business Ethics & Transparency

### Description of the Management System for Prevention of Corruption and Bribery (EM-EP-510a.1)

The entity shall describe its management system for prevention of corruption and bribery throughout the value chain.

### Production in Countries with 20 Lowest Rankings in Corruption Perceptions Index (EM-EP-510a.2)

The entity shall disclose the production, in BOE, in countries that have the 20 lowest rankings in Transparency International's Corruption Perceptions Index.

## Management of the Legal & Regulatory Environment

### Discussion of Corporate Positions Related to Government Regulations (EM-EP-530a.1)

The entity shall discuss its positions related to government regulations and/or policy proposals that address environmental and social factors affecting the industry.
